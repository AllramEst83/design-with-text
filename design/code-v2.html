02. Copy & Hyperlinks

This paragraph demonstrates the Newsreader body text style. We prioritize legibility and breathability, using a 1.6 line-height to evoke the feeling of a morning broadsheet. It is vital that all hypertext links remain clearly distinguished from the rest of the body text to maintain historical web continuity.

When citing external sources or internal cross-references, we use the Primary Hyperlink Blue (#4B4BD4), ensuring it passes WCAG AA contrast against our warm paper-toned background.
